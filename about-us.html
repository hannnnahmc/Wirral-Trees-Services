About Wirral Tree Services

Wirral Tree Services is a Wirral-based tree surgery, garden maintenance, and vegetation management specialist, offering cost-effective workmanship to the residential and commercial sectors. We are committed to customer satisfaction, reliability, and forming long-term working relationships with our customers. Our staff are fully trained to modern standards and our duty of care to preserve the tree is always taken into consideration. All trees and hedges react differently to certain types of pruning. With our knowledge and experience, we give our customers peace of mind. Be safe in the knowledge that your tree or hedge is being pruned in a safe and sustainable way, safeguarding their health for years to come.
