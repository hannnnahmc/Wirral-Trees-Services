Covid-19 - Social Distancing

Due to the contagious risk and spread of the Coronavirus (COVID 19) we are implementing with immediate effect, safeguarding procedure to protect our clients and staff whilst maintaining an operational status. Below are changes to our procedures to ensure we can continue to provide our Services:

  1. During our site visits, we will now, not be meeting face to face. Work specifications can be relayed through telephone or emails etc.
  2. We will be maintaining a zero-interaction policy whilst we are carrying out works. Unfortunately, this means we will currently be declining drinks, biscuits, etc. This is to remove the risk of contact and ultimately possible infection.
  3. Should anyone approach us whilst working, we will ask you to stop 2-3m away from any employee to ensure that we follow preventive measure laid out by the government.
  4. We will not be shaking hands or elbow bumps as an extra precaution.
  5. Please do not panic, we have a very low level of human interaction during our working day, due to the nature of our work and we can fully operate at normal capacity.

We are thoroughly following government and medical guidance. Should we become infected as a company we will immediately close, inform clients and reschedule works.

Regards, Matthew Monaghan

About us

Founded in 2015, Wirral Tree Services specialises in tree work and arboriculture in Wirral, Merseyside, and the surrounding areas. From big jobs like tree feeling and sectional dismantling, to simple pruning or reductions work, we provide comprehensive tree surgery work for all our customers.

We don’t just work on trees either – we also offer hedge cutting, shrub trimming, grass cutting and much more. Plus, we collect all waste and create quality seasoned firewood and renewable energy from the leftover wood. Don’t settle for less – get quality tree work at an affordable price.
